Woynishet Mesfin

Born in Addis Ababa, Woynishet works exclusively with charcoal, graphite and white ink. Her monochromatic palette reveals raw emotion — faces, highlands, and silence. She graduated from the Alle School of Fine Arts and has exhibited in group shows across Ethiopia.

Each original work is sold at 500 birr (unframed). Commission requests are welcome through the contact channels.

— W. Mesfin
